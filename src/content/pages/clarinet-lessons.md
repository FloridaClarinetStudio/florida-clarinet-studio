## Lessons Tailored to the Student

Robin works with students who want to make All-State or All-County, improve solo and ensemble results, win an audition, move into a higher band, or simply play better with more confidence.

Lessons are built around each student's goals, current level, school expectations, practice habits, and musical interests.

## What Lessons Cover

- Clarinet tone production and air support
- Finger technique and clean facility
- Articulation, rhythm, and sight reading
- Scales, etudes, solos, and ensemble excerpts
- Reed, mouthpiece, ligature, and instrument guidance
- Audition preparation and performance confidence

## Lesson Formats

- Lessons at Robin's Palm Bay teaching location, Friday through Sunday
- Travel lessons Monday through Thursday, based on availability
- Online lessons through Zoom, Discord, or WhatsApp
- School clinics, sectionals, and audition preparation
