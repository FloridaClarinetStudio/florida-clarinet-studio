## Practical Support for School Music Programs

Robin works with band directors who want focused woodwind support, stronger student preparation, and practical reinforcement for the musical goals already happening in the classroom.

Robin Ryon can support clarinet and saxophone students through clinics, sectionals, audition preparation, listening guidance, and leadership coaching.

## Support Options

- Clarinet sectionals
- Saxophone and woodwind sectionals
- Full-band clinics
- All-County and All-State audition preparation
- Solo and ensemble preparation
- Leadership training and student confidence coaching
- Listening lists and model sound guidance

## A Teaching Partner for Band Directors

The goal is to make your ensemble stronger, not to compete with your program. Students receive practical help with tone, technique, rhythm, style, and preparation so they return to rehearsal more confident and more useful to the section.
