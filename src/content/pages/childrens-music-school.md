## Gear Should Help the Student, Not Fight the Student

The right reed, mouthpiece, ligature, and stand can make daily practice easier and more consistent. Gear does not replace fundamentals, but the wrong setup can make good habits harder to build.

## Recommended Categories

### Reeds

Students should use reliable reeds that match their mouthpiece, experience level, and current embouchure development. Clarinet and saxophone reed strength should be chosen carefully instead of guessed.

### Mouthpieces

A step-up mouthpiece can make tone, response, and intonation more stable. Students should get guidance before buying, because the best option depends on age, instrument, and goals.

### Accessories

Useful accessories include a sturdy music stand, cork grease, a safe instrument stand, reed care tools, and a metronome/tuner.

## Gear Notes

The blog contains individual gear notes for reeds, mouthpieces, ligatures, stands, and accessories. Treat them as a starting point, then ask for guidance before making a major purchase.
