## Listening Builds Sound

Students improve faster when they listen to excellent players. A strong listening habit helps students internalize tone, phrasing, style, articulation, and musical direction.

## Clarinet Artists to Know

### Harold Wright

Harold Wright was principal clarinetist of the Boston Symphony Orchestra from 1970 to 1993. His playing is an essential sound model for orchestral clarinet tone, elegance, and phrasing.

### Robert Marcellus

Robert Marcellus was principal clarinetist of the Cleveland Orchestra from 1953 to 1973 and remains one of the most important American clarinet teachers and performers.

## How to Use This List

- Listen for sound before trying to copy notes.
- Pick one phrase and sing it before playing it.
- Notice articulation, vibrato or lack of vibrato, phrase endings, and dynamic shape.
- Revisit the same recordings as your playing matures.
