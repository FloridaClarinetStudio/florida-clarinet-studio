## Saxophone Lessons With a Clear Plan

Robin offers alto and tenor saxophone instruction for students who want focused, practical coaching. Lessons are built around the student's current level, school expectations, musical interests, and goals.

Students can work on fundamentals, jazz and classical style, school ensemble music, solo and ensemble preparation, audition material, and the practice habits that make progress more consistent.

## What Saxophone Lessons Cover

- Tone, air support, and embouchure stability
- Finger technique, scales, and facility
- Articulation, rhythm, sight reading, and style
- School band, jazz band, and solo repertoire
- Reed, mouthpiece, ligature, and instrument guidance
- Audition preparation and performance confidence

## Lesson Formats

- Lessons at Robin's Palm Bay teaching location, Friday through Sunday
- Travel lessons Monday through Thursday, based on availability
- Online lessons through Zoom, Discord, or WhatsApp
- School clinics, woodwind sectionals, and audition preparation
